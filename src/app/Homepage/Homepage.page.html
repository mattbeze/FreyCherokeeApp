This application is a prototype designed to help people learn Spanish in a contextual setting. We use the scenario of ordering a coffee to introduce beginners to the language in a fast and easy method.

This app has an example video to view and follow with transcriptions that won't overwhelm with complicated language. Take a look at the glossary for extra terms, or to specify your own order of coffee and any extra items as necessary.
